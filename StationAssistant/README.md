# Station Assistant

Quality-of-life mod for **Vanguard Galaxy** that handles chores at the station for you.

## What it does

- **Decoy transponders** — buys them when you dock, activates one when you undock.
- **Auto-sell** — sells surplus gear (turrets, modules, boosters) by rarity and level, and keeps
  anything you flag.
- **Ammo valet** — loads the ammo your guns use and stashes the rest.
- **Loadouts** — saves a ship's current gear setup and re-applies it later (shows you a preview
  first, and leaves slots you didn't save alone).

## Installing

Install from **Nexus Mods** with a mod manager (like Vortex), or extract the zip into your game
folder. Restart the game.

## Using it

Press **F7** for the settings window, with tabs for Decoy, Auto-sell, Ammo, and Loadouts. Quick
keys: **F8** sells, **F9** handles ammo. All keys can be changed in the **Hotkeys** tab.
