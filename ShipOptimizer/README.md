# Ship Optimizer

A web app for **Vanguard Galaxy** that looks at your ship, your inventory, and the station shops,
suggests a better loadout, and applies it for you. It runs through the **[Hypercom](../Hypercom/)**
mod.

## What it does

- **Gear** — for each slot, shows your current fit next to a suggested one. Suggestions only ever
  upgrade unless you change the type yourself. Filter by type, damage, or aspect.
- **Officers** — ranks and assigns your crew by the skills you care about.
- **Boosters** — picks the strongest boosters for your slots.
- **Opportunities** — scans your inventory and the station shops for items that beat what you have
  equipped.
- **Summary** — review every suggested change in one place and **apply** it to your docked ship in
  one click, with undo. Save and reload named loadout presets.

## Using it

You don't run this yourself. Install **Hypercom**, start the game, and the Ship Optimizer opens in
your browser.

- Applying changes needs your ship **docked**; while undocked, gear is shown read-only.
- The app refreshes on its own when you dock, undock, install gear, or switch ships. An **ECHO**
  badge shows when autopilot is flying.
- Presets are kept per playthrough. The **Tools** menu (⋯) can export and import loadouts.
